3 Steps to Manifesting Anything You Want

  1. 1. Ask

    2. Step 1 is ask. Often times we spend a great deal talking about what we don't want and we are really good about doing this in detail. This can come in the form of complaining, or describing our current reality. Some examples are talking about how you never have money, how broke you are, complaining about the economy, etc. While all of this may be true now it isn't putting a great deal of energy into what you do want. So what do you want? Write it down without negatives. Meaning, if you want more money, write how much money you want, don't write down that you don't want to be broke anymore, this only perpetuates the energy of being broke and not having enough.


  2. 2. It is Given (no work on your part)


    3. I like to describe this step as having fun and living your life. By the time you get to Step 2 you already know what you want, you wrote it down. As Abraham Hicks says you are now beating the drum of what you would like to receive more of rather than what you're lacking. You have clarity. Step 2 is about staying in the energy of clarity. Knowing you get to have what you want and that it isn't your job to stay caught up in the energy of "how". How it's going to come, when it's going to come, etc. All of those details is staying attached rather than detached. Step 2 is about knowing you get to have it and focusing on things in your life that make you feel good now. You're in the in between phase, which is the juiciest phase. You know it's coming, you're living your best life in the meantime as if it is already here. You're excited.


  3. 3. Be Open to Receiving

    4. Step 3 is watching how you feel, leaning in to what feels good. We tend to get into inspired action during step 3 and "follow our impluses or nudges". This is about being open to what you wrote down coming into your reality. Maybe it is an idea, a project you've been putting off that you all of a sudden have more inspiration to start. Or maybe something unexpected comes into your world without you having to do anything for it. This can be a refund check in the mail that you didn't expect. A raise at work, other good news or opportunities. Its noticing that things can happen in our experience from being more clear, enjoying our life, showing up when inspiration hits, etc. This is where you being to enjoy the fruitian of your manifestations. Then we can go back to step 1 over and over again. We continue to ask, get clear and know that it is coming.
