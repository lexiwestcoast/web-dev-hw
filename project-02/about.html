Why I chose this Project

3 Steps to Manifesting Anything You Want

I have been using manifestation for the last 4 years pretty seriously.

I think a lot of people don't think that it works, even though my life is proof that it does! In my early twenties I had a handful of mentors who greatly impacted my life and taught me a lot of the practices I still use today. Some of these mentors I had never met and others I worked closely with. I've learned that life doesn't always get better by just letting time pass by, but when we are clear about what we want. My favorite quote from one of my mentors Lisa Grossmann is, "if you don't master the law of specificity, then you will master in mediocrity." That's why getting clear about what I want in my life, how I want to feel and being an active particiapte in my life is so important to me. It doesn't have to be called "manifestation" if that doesn't resonate with you. Whatever you want to call it, it works.

My favorite manifestation teachers include Amanda Frances. Her course, Money Mentality Makeover changed my life. I've consumed all of her free content on social media, including her meditations on Youtube. I also recommend Monica Yates. She teaches a lot on abundance and using manifesation with your health / body. I really enjoyed her Be a Lover, Not a Mother course. She specializes in emobdying your feminine energy and not emasculating men.

Lastly, my go-to foundational resource is Abraham Hicks. They have books such as, Ask and it is Given (which is what these three steps are from) and an endless library of short talks on manifestation, Law of Attraction and how to use these principles in your life.
