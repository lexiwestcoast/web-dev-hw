My Story

From Athlete to Drug Addict to Entreprenuer and Personal Trainer

Alexis Olson

CEO & Founder of Alive & Aligned, LLC

Growing up, I loved sports but was a mediocre athlete. Even though I wasn’t leading the team or breaking records, I was having fun and felt alive while competing. Sometimes, life changes and I started down a path that I knew I had to correct. I was quickly becoming unhealthy and unrecognizable in the mirror. It was at that moment that I decided to change my life trajectory and recommit to my fitness journey. However, I had a lot of work to do from a childhood eating disorder coming back to chronic fatigue, anxiety and sometimes depression. I knew there had to be something going on so I began researching and learned about Adaptogens and holistic approaches to healing myself inside and out. You know what? My education and hard work paid off and I am now the woman that I want to be. It was with this personal success that I realized I wasn’t the only one in this position. There are many more like me. That is the moment I decided to become a trainer to help others change their life trajectory and become the person they want to be.
